About

My research interests include high-dimensional clustering, fuzzy multi-attribute group decision making (MAGDM), and intelligent optimization for complex financial decision making and urban analytics. I focus on improving robustness, discrimination ability, and ranking stability in high-dimensional expert information fusion.

Research Interests

  • High-dimensional clustering and forecasting
  • Fuzzy sets: intuitionistic fuzzy numbers, neutrosophic sets, picture fuzzy sets
  • Optimization-based aggregation operators (PGSA, NSGA-II, PSO, etc.)
  • Financial decision support and stock analytics
