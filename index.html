I am a graduate student at Beijing Information Science and Technology University (BISTU). My research focuses on high-dimensional clustering, fuzzy multi-attribute group decision making, and intelligent optimization, with applications in urban planning and financial decision making.

My current research interests include high-dimensional data modeling, aggregation operators based on triangular intuitionistic fuzzy numbers, and optimization-driven decision frameworks such as PGSA and NSGA-II. I aim to improve robustness, discrimination ability, and interpretability in complex decision-making scenarios.

Research Interests

  • High-dimensional clustering and representation learning
  • Multi-attribute group decision making (MAGDM)
  • Fuzzy sets and intuitionistic fuzzy modeling
  • Intelligent optimization algorithms (PGSA, NSGA-II)
  • Financial data analysis and forecasting
